About Boardgame Binder

Boardgame Binder has access to nearly 10,000 of your favorite boardgames! Find and track games with filters including number of players, playing time, and ratings per BoardGameGeek

[Instructions how to use app]

[Matthew Lee/link to portfolio] created this app to showcase his full stack web dev skills. Boardgame Binder uses SQL, Express, Angular, and Node.js

Front End

Angular 5 template driven forms comprise most of the front-end. Guards and validation are in place to protect routes and data injection. Minimal CSS and Angular Material are used, with mobile-first design.

Back End

The back end uses SQLite because of the dataset source. Three tables are used in this relational DB in order to add boardgames to user's profile.

Incomplete features includes editing User Profile information, social networking plugins, and email intigration such as confirming account creation and "forgot password" ability.
